All Inclusive Package Deal

Once you reach the wonderful location of La Pecora Ladra, in the Italian region of Maremma, everything (except for your drinks) will be included in your week-long experience.

Accommodation in a single-occupancy and very spacious room with en-suite bathroom, breakfast, lunch and dinner, a professional model, hair and make up artist,styling, ground transportations to the different shooting locations, Profoto lighting equipment and a full team to take care of you and your needs.

Your coach and tutor for the week is Andrea Belluso who will not only share some of his lighting techniques for

fashion and beauty photography, both on location and in the studio, but who will also give you a personal evaluation of the images that you will be taking during the week.

All you have to think about and to take care of is your way to and from La Pecora Ladra, your camera and lenses, your computer and, if you like your wine with or without your meal, that will be charged as an extra cost.

Together we will be shooting in rural settings of the Maremma region as well as a couple of locations in the eternal city of Rome.

La Pecora Ladra

"La pecora ladra" is a holiday farm situated in the southern Maremma region, on a hill close to the sea, with views of Argentario promontory and Giannutri island. The farm holding extends over twenty hectares, a sea of green formed by age-old olive trees.
